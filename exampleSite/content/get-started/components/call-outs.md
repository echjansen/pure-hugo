We support these broad categories of callouts:

- Alerts: Note, Tip, Important, Warning, Caution

## Examples

> [!NOTE]
>
> Do we have your attention?

> [!TIP]
>
> For a smaller base image, use `alpine`.

> [!IMPORTANT]
>
> Never reveal your password to anyone.
> That includes us!

> [!WARNING]
>
> Removing files
>

> [!CAUTION]
>
> Here be dragons.
